How to Use This Website

  • Home Page: Displays the welcome message and links to other pages.
  • Gallery Page: Shows images and a student data table. You can hover over images to zoom and view detailed alt-text.
  • Booking Page: Fill in your name, choose your major, select your gender, mark your hobbies, and leave a comment. Fields marked with a * are required.
  • Help Page: You are here! This page provides guidance on how to use every part of the site.
  • About Page: Learn about the developer (Ceyni Adan Ahmed).
